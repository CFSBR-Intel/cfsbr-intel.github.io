About CFSBR

The Centre for Fintech & Strategic Business Research (CFSBR) is an "Intellectual Entrepreneurship" hub operating at the intersection of Economics (Context), Engineering (Tool), and Business Strategy (Sustainability). We turn academic rigor into market-ready intelligence for banks, fintechs, and policymakers.

Journal Article Employment

Investigation of Market Opportunities for BBA Graduates

Mohammed Safwan Ahmed, MD. Safwan Jumael Khan, Hameem Ahmed Chowdhury, Mehraj Ahmed

International Journal of Sustainability and Multidisciplinary Research (IJSMR) • 2025

A comprehensive case study examining employment market opportunities and career prospects for BBA graduates in Sylhet Metropolitan Area, analyzing industry demands and skill gaps.

Journal Article Monetary Policy

Examining the Role of Repo Rate in Controlling Inflation

Mashrura Meshkat Punno, Md Golam Mubasshir Rafi

International Journal of Sustainability and Multidisciplinary Research (IJSMR) • 2025

This comprehensive study evaluates the effectiveness of repo rate adjustments as a primary monetary policy instrument for inflation control, examining the dynamics of proportional relationships and limitations in volatile economic conditions.

Conference Paper Digital Finance

Exploring MFS Adoption & Basic Financial Literacy Among Market Merchants

Md Golam Mubasshir Rafi, Israt Zarin Ruponty

International Conference on Financial Literacy and Economic Wellbeing (ICFLEW), BRAC University • 2025

Comprehensive study investigating MFS adoption patterns and basic financial literacy levels among market merchants in Sylhet, identifying barriers and proposing solutions for enhanced digital financial inclusion in local markets.

Green Finance & Climate Risk: Challenges and Opportunities for Sustainable Growth

S. M. Mosaddad Ali, Sabikunnahar Mahi

LURS 2nd Student Research Conference, Leading University Research Society (LURS) • 2025
