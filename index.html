About CFSBR

The Centre for Fintech & Strategic Business Research (CFSBR) is a hybrid research firm and think-tank operating at the critical intersection of Economics (The Context), Engineering (The Backbone), Business Strategy (The Sustainability), and Law & Policy (The Framework). We turn academic rigor into market-ready intelligence for banks, fintechs, and policymakers.

What We Do

Bridging the gap between data, technology, and policy to deliver actionable financial intelligence.

Deep-Dive Market Intelligence

Going beyond traditional surveys: we use data science and economic modeling to analyze consumer behavior, inflation trends, and digital adoption patterns in emerging markets—delivering the raw data banks and foreign investors need.

Startup Blueprint & MVP Design

A comprehensive launchpad for fintech founders: we design sustainable business models, map technical architectures (Engineering), and validate concepts before market entry to reduce failure risk.

Regulatory & Policy Advisory

Navigating financial regulations with confidence: we align innovations with Central Bank guidelines and legal frameworks to ensure compliance, safety, and long-term viability.

Corporate Training & Capacity Building

Hands-on workshops to bridge the skill gap: Financial Data Analytics (SPSS/Python), Research Methodology, and Fintech Fundamentals for students and professionals.

R&D as a Service

Your external innovation lab: we handle product research, feasibility testing, and impact analysis—delivering innovation faster and more efficiently for banks and NGOs.

Fintech Fellowship

Nurturing the next generation of thought leaders through a structured mentorship program where undergraduate researchers work on live projects—bridging academic theory and industry application.

Featured Intelligence

Transforming raw data into strategic insights: delivering market intelligence, technical innovations, and policy frameworks that drive financial inclusion and sustainable growth across emerging markets.
