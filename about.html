About AIBARS

The Army Institute of Business Administration Research Society (AIBARS) is dedicated to cultivating a vibrant research ecosystem at AIBA Sylhet. We empower students to explore innovative ideas, develop critical thinking skills, and contribute meaningfully to academic discourse and real-world problem-solving.

Research Excellence

Fostering a culture of academic rigor and innovative research methodologies

Collaborative Community

Recognition & Publishing

Supporting publication in esteemed journals and academic conferences

Sustainable Growth

Driving research that creates lasting impact on global challenges
